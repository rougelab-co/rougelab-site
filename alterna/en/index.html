Who we are

A place where
color speaks

Rouge Lab was born from the belief that creating with your hands can transform you. We are a studio-school in the heart of Lima where artists, curious minds, and teams discover their own visual language.

Our workshops combine technique, experimentation, and plenty of color. Art is not a talent you are simply born with, it is a practice that can be cultivated.

  • ●
    Process over outcome

    We value the creative journey as much as the finished piece.

  • ●
    Small groups

    No more than 10 people per workshop for close guidance.

  • ●
    Materials included

    Everything you need is already waiting for you in the studio.

Why art helps

Artistic practice also supports well-being, confidence, and connection.

It is not just intuition. Evidence gathered by the WHO across thousands of studies links artistic practice with well-being, emotional regulation, social connection, and lifelong learning.

Regulates and focuses

Creating with your hands helps quiet mental noise, sustain attention, and find a real pause in the middle of everyday life.

Builds confidence

Exploring materials, making mistakes, and trying again develops creative autonomy and a kinder relationship with error.

Connects people

Shared art-making encourages empathy, conversation, and belonging in children, adults, and teams alike.

Gift Card

Gift a workshop,
gift a space of your own

Some gifts last a moment and others open an experience. At Rouge Lab, you can gift a workshop spot as a gift card so someone you care about can pause, explore their creativity, and take home something made with their own hands.

It works like any other studio offering: choose the workshop or amount you want to gift and we turn it into a special invitation for children, adults, or even a shared experience.

A meaningful gift

You are not only giving an object, you are giving time, attention, and an experience worth remembering.

Flexible and personal

It can be for a specific workshop, a free amount, or a special date such as a birthday, Mother's Day, or a team celebration.

For all ages

It is a beautiful way to invite someone to create, whether it is a curious child, an adult reconnecting with art, or someone in need of a different kind of pause.
