Frequently asked questions

What if I've never drawn in my life?

Perfect! This workshop is designed from scratch. No prior experience needed — just a willingness to try.

What if I can't attend live?

You'll receive the full recording with lifetime access. Watch it as many times as you want, at your own pace.

What materials do I need?

A 2B pencil, eraser, colored pencils, and bond or sketch paper. Things you probably already have at home. We'll send you a detailed guide when you sign up.

What if I don't like it?

You have a 100% guarantee. If you finish the class and aren't satisfied, we'll refund your money — no questions asked.

Can I take the workshop from my phone?

Yes, but we recommend a tablet or computer to see the drawing details better.

Absolutely! The class is at 7:00 pm Lima time (UTC-5). You can switch the price to USD on this page.
